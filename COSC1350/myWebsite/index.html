I have been mountain biking for over forty years but I was starting to think I was going to have to give it up. It was getting too hard to make it up the hills and long rides were getting harder and harder to do. Then I got an ebike. It changed my life and made mountain biking fun again. I made this page and my youtube channel to share my experience.
